SoundTræk

A living explanation layer for the codebase

SoundTræk answers one question: what song was playing at each moment of a run — and what does that look like on a map?

It takes your historical running files and Spotify listening history, joins them by timestamp, and renders a GPS route where each segment is colored by the album art of whatever was playing.
